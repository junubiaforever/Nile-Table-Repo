What We Are

Where culture
meets connection

The Nile Table is a professional gathering series built for South Sudanese individuals who are building something and need a room that understands the journey. Each edition is an invitation to gather, engage, and celebrate the beauty of our shared stories and experiences.

Culture — rooted in who we are
Conversation — honest, generative, real
Connection — relationships that last beyond the room
"We shared our real-life experiences and discovered that our journeys are far more connected than we often realize."

— Founder
